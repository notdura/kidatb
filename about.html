About Me

Biography

"If it's to be, it's up to me"

I live by these words; These words guide my approach to work and life. I believe in taking initiative and creating opportunities instead of waiting for them to appear.

My passion for UI and UX design started long before I knew the terms. Back in the early 2010s, I was building websites and forums from scratch, driven by a love for creating functional and beautiful online spaces. This passion only grew stronger during my high school years, where I studied Aesthetics & Media, giving me a foundation in visual communication and storytelling.

Today, I specialize in UI/UX design with a strong passion for prototyping - seeing my work become interactive is the favourite part of my work. I also love experimenting with color, styling, and interactivity to create polished, intuitive designs. To me it's important that the work I do improves life in some way, either through apps that simplify daily tasks or that entertain and inspire.

On my free-time, I stay creative by working with clay, crocheting, painting and reading books. Ask me about my current project(s)!

Versatile, dedicated & ambitious,

are just a few words people have used to describe me, but they’re only part of the story. :)
